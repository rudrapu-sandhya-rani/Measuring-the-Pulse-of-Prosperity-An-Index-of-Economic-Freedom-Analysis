Introduction

Economic freedom is a vital foundation of prosperity, as it empowers individuals to control their own labor, property, and financial resources. Countries with higher levels of economic freedom tend to achieve faster economic growth, higher standards of living, and greater innovation. This project aims to measure and analyze economic freedom across 185 countries using a comprehensive index.

The index incorporates multiple dimensions of freedom, including property rights, government integrity, fiscal health, business freedom, monetary stability, and trade openness. By aggregating these factors, we can develop a holistic view of economic freedom and its correlation with national prosperity and social well-being.

Objectives: The main objectives of this analysis are:

  • To evaluate the distribution of economic freedom globally
  • To identify patterns and outliers among countries
  • To study the relationship between economic freedom and growth indicators such as GDP, unemployment, and inflation
  • To present actionable insights for policymakers, researchers, and investors

Methodology: Data was collected from credible economic and governance reports, then visualized through interactive Tableau dashboards and storytelling features. This data-driven approach ensures transparency and encourages users to explore and interpret the data for themselves.

Insightful Story

Conclusion

Measuring the pulse of prosperity through this Economic Freedom Index helps decision-makers prioritize reforms and investments that strengthen freedom, opportunity, and innovation. This project hopes to spark further research, debate, and policy change that promotes inclusive and sustainable growth worldwide.
